# Hospital Management System Analysis

## Overview
This project presents an end-to-end business analysis for a hospital management system designed to improve operational efficiency, reduce manual paperwork, and centralise patient information across hospital departments.

## Business Problem
As patient volume increased, managing appointments, records, billing, and departmental coordination became more complex and inefficient. Manual processes created delays, increased administrative effort, and reduced visibility across hospital operations.

## Objective
To define a hospital management solution that supports patient registration, appointment scheduling, billing, bed occupancy tracking, reporting, and interdepartmental coordination.

## Tools and Techniques Used
- Business requirements analysis
- Stakeholder analysis
- Functional and non-functional requirements
- Process modelling
- Data flow diagrams
- Workflow design
- Scope definition
- Mock screen design

## Approach
- Analysed the current operational challenges and business needs
- Identified key stakeholders across management, clinical teams, administration, finance, and external entities
- Defined in-scope and out-of-scope items
- Produced functional and non-functional requirements
- Created proposed workflows for patients, doctors, nurses, and system interactions
- Developed data flow diagrams and supporting system models
- Designed mock screens for dashboards, appointment scheduling, and sign-in pages
## Key Deliverables
- Business requirements document
- Stakeholder analysis
- Functional requirements
- Non-functional requirements
- In-scope and out-of-scope definition
- Proposed workflow design
- Data flow diagrams
- Patient admission flowchart
- Mock screens for the system

## Key Features of the Proposed System
- Patient registration and unique ID generation
- Online appointment booking
- SMS and email appointment reminders
- Patient check-in and record updates
- Bed occupancy tracking
- Consolidated patient billing
- Insurance detail storage for claim processing
- Reporting on appointments, revenue, and occupancy

## Business Impact
This proposed solution improves operational efficiency, supports better decision-making, reduces paperwork, enhances patient data accessibility, and provides a scalable foundation for future hospital growth.
